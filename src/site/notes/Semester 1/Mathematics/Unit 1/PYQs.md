### Infinite Series & Convergence
1. **Convergence Test**: Test the convergence of the following series:
   $$\frac{1^2}{4^2} + \frac{1^2 \cdot 5^2}{4^2 \cdot 8^2} + \frac{1^2 \cdot 5^2 \cdot 9^2}{4^2 \cdot 8^2 \cdot 12^2} + \dots$$
2. **Trigonometric Series**: Discuss the convergence of the series $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n} \tan\left(\frac{1}{n}\right)$.
3. **Power Series**: Test the convergence of the series $\sum \frac{n^2-1}{n^2+1} x^n$ for positive values of $x$.

## ðŸ“ Selected Previous Year Questions

### 1. Casting and Manufacturing
1. **Sand Casting**: Explain the steps involved in making a sand mould with a neat flowchart or diagram.
2. **Casting Defects**: List common casting defects (e.g., Blow holes, Shrinkage cavities). Explain their causes and remedies.
3. **Patterns**: What is a Pattern? detailed the different types of allowances provided on patterns (Shrinkage, Draft, Machining).

### 2. Joining Processes
1. **Welding**: Differentiate between Arc Welding and Gas Welding.
2. **Soldering vs Brazing**: Compare and contrast Soldering, Brazing, and Welding.
3. **Gas Welding Flames**: Explain the three types of Oxy-acetylene flames (Neutral, Oxidizing, Reducing) and their applications.
4. **Advanced Welding**: Explain the principle and working of TIG (GTAW) and MIG (GMAW) welding processes.

### 3. Metal Forming
1. **Forging vs Rolling**: Differentiate between Forging and Rolling processes.
2. **Hot vs Cold Working**: Distinguish between Hot Working and Cold Working processes based on recrystallization temperature.

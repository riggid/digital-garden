*   A) curl of magnetic field = $\mu_0 j$

### Question 4
A photon of frequency $\nu$ scatters from target at rest and moves in a direction making an angle 60$^\circ$ with the incident beam direction. If the frequency of scattered photon is half that of incident photon, calculate the frequency of the incident photon.
*   A) $25 \times 10^{20}$ Hz
*   B) $2.5 \times 10^{20}$ Hz
*   C) $250 \times 10^{20}$ Hz
*   D) $0.25 \times 10^{20}$ Hz

<details>
<summary><strong>Check Answer</strong></summary>

> **Correct Answer: B) $2.5 \times 10^{20}$ Hz**
> Using $\frac{c}{\nu'} - \frac{c}{\nu} = \lambda_C(1-\cos 60^\circ) \implies \frac{c}{\nu/2} - \frac{c}{\nu} = \frac{h}{m_e c}(0.5) \implies \frac{c}{\nu} = \frac{h}{2m_e c} \implies \nu = \frac{2m_e c^2}{h}$.
> $\nu \approx \frac{2(0.511 \text{ MeV})}{4.135 \times 10^{-15} \text{ eV s}} \approx 2.47 \times 10^{20}$ Hz.
</details>

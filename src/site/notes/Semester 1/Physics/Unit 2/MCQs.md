### Question 2
In a 2D square well, which state is higher in energy?
*   A) (2, 2)
*   B) (3, 1)
*   C) Both are equal
*   D) Depends on height of the potential

<details>
<summary><strong>Check Answer</strong></summary>

> **Correct Answer: B) (3, 1)**
> $2^2+2^2 = 8$. $3^2+1^2 = 10$. 10 > 8.
</details>

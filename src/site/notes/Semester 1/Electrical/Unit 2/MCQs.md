# Unit 2: Single-Phase AC Circuits - MCQs

## AC Fundamentals

### Question 1
The frequency of AC supply in India is:
*   A) 50 Hz
*   B) 60 Hz
*   C) 100 Hz
*   D) 220 Hz

<details>
<summary><strong>Check Answer</strong></summary>

> **Correct Answer: A) 50 Hz**
</details>

### Question 2
The RMS value of AC is also called:
*   A) Peak value
*   B) Average value
*   C) Effective value
*   D) Instantaneous value

<details>
<summary><strong>Check Answer</strong></summary>

> **Correct Answer: C) Effective value**
</details>

## Phasors & Impedance

### Question 1
Impedance in an AC circuit is the combination of:
*   A) Resistance only
*   B) Reactance only
*   C) Resistance and Reactance
*   D) Voltage and Current

<details>
<summary><strong>Check Answer</strong></summary>

> **Correct Answer: C) Resistance and Reactance**
> 
> *Z = R + jX*
</details>

### Question 2
In a purely inductive circuit, current:
*   A) Leads voltage by 90°
*   B) Lags voltage by 90°
*   C) Is in phase with voltage
*   D) Leads voltage by 45°

<details>
<summary><strong>Check Answer</strong></summary>

> **Correct Answer: B) Lags voltage by 90°**
</details>

## Power in AC Circuits

### Question 1
Power factor is defined as:
*   A) cosθ
*   B) sinθ
*   C) tanθ
*   D) secθ

<details>
<summary><strong>Check Answer</strong></summary>

> **Correct Answer: A) cosθ**
> 
> *Power factor = cos(phase angle)*
</details>

### Question 2
Reactive power is measured in:
*   A) Watts (W)
*   B) Volt-Amperes (VA)
*   C) Volt-Amperes Reactive (VAR)
*   D) Joules (J)

<details>
<summary><strong>Check Answer</strong></summary>

> **Correct Answer: C) Volt-Amperes Reactive (VAR)**
</details>
